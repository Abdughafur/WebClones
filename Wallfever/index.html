🎨 What is Wallfever?
Wallfever is a wallpaper app that features beautifully handcrafted Material You wallpapers inspired by Google’s colorful design language — vibrant, clean, and uniquely human-made.
📱 Which platforms is Wallfever available on?
Wallfever is currently available as a standalone app on Android. For iOS users, it’s available as a dedicated collection inside the Lagguy Wallpapers app.
🖼️ Does the iOS version include all Wallfever wallpapers?
No. The iOS version inside the Lagguy Wallpapers app includes select wallpapers from Wallfever, especially newer and curated ones. Not all Android wallpapers are included yet.
💸 Is Wallfever free to use?
Yes, Wallfever offers a free tier on Android with a selection of wallpapers. To unlock the full handcrafted collection, users can make a one-time purchase. On iOS, the Wallfever collection is accessible only to Yearly and Lifetime Pro users of the Lagguy Wallpapers app.
🔗 Will my purchase in Wallfever work inside Lagguy Wallpapers?
No. Wallfever and Lagguy Wallpapers are separate apps. Purchases made in Wallfever do not carry over to the Lagguy Wallpapers app.
📲 Can I use Wallfever wallpapers on iPhone?
Yes, through the Lagguy Wallpapers iOS app — the Wallfever collection is available to users on a Yearly or Lifetime Pro plan.
🚀 Will Lagguy Wallpapers launch on Android too?
Yes! Lagguy Wallpapers is coming soon to Android and will include the full Wallfever collection along with other exclusive collections like Smudgess, TeamPixel, and Abstracto.
🧠 Who creates the wallpapers in Wallfever?
All wallpapers in Wallfever are handmade. Each design is carefully crafted to reflect Google’s Material You aesthetic with vibrant gradients, shapes, and forms.
